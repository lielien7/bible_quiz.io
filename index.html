Bible Quiz

How to play? It's easy and fun...

Answer to Bible's questions and find out how well do you know the Bible!

You will have 10 seconds for each question to choose your answer. After the time is up, the next question will load. So, stay tuned, be quick and pay attention to the details.

You will receive one point for each correct answer, and zero points for a wrong one. If you are right on the question, the option you choose will be green and you will get a point. If your answer is not correct, it will turn into red and no extra points for you.

When you have finished the Bible's quiz, your total score will indicate how well you know Bible. Enjoy!
